MS-CIT Course

Maharashtra State Certificate in Information Technology

About MS-CIT

MS-CIT is an Information Technology (IT) literacy course started by MKCL in the year 2001. It is the most popular IT Literacy course in Maharashtra, designed to equip students with essential computer skills needed in today's digital world.

Course Duration

The MS-CIT course spans over 3 months (144 hours total) and includes:

  • 50 hours of classroom content (1 hour per day)
  • 50 hours of lab content on computer and smartphone (1 hour per day)
  • 44 hours of book reading, self-study, revision, and practice

Course Syllabus

The MS-CIT curriculum covers a wide range of skills and topics:

  • SCRATCH Programming
  • Basic Programming Concepts
  • Introduction to C Programming
  • Introduction to Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)
  • Microsoft Office Applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Internet and Email
  • Digital Financial Transactions
  • Cyber Security Awareness
  • Social Media Usage

Benefits of MS-CIT Certification

  • Recognized qualification for government and private sector jobs
  • Essential for various competitive exams
  • Improves digital literacy and employability
  • Provides practical skills for daily digital tasks
  • Builds confidence in using technology

KLIC Courses

Knowledge Lit Career Development Programs

About KLIC

KLiC stands for Knowledge Lit Career. KLiC series of courses serve as a bridge from college to careers. MKCL, under its KLiC brand, offers employability skills development modules as KLiC Certificate Courses.

Course Duration

KLIC courses typically run for 120 hours and can be covered in:

  • 1 month (4 weeks) intensive program
  • 2 months (8 weeks) regular program
  • 3 months (12 weeks) extended program

Some specialized courses are available in 60-hour formats as well.
